How to play :

Tic Tac Toe is a fun game for two players! You and your opponent will take turns to mark the spaces in a 3x3 grid. The first player will begin with an X while the opponent is an O. The player will take turns until one succeeds in placing three respective marks in a horizontal, vertical, or diagonal row. The player with three respective marks wins the game receiving an extra point. If both player fails to mark three respective marks, and the board is full, the game is tied.

Server: Player will wait for another player to join and begin the game. Players can reset the board to reset the score. Client: Players can select which server to join and begin the game. Player can restart the board.
